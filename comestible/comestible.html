About this collection

Kale contains nearly twice the amount of vitamin K as most other vegetables found within the cruciferous or cabbage family. Vitamin K plays an important role in bone health, so our collection is extremely beneficial to strengthening nails and keeping them tough like bones. These beautiful colors presented within our collection our based on the beneficial nutrients found in kale.
